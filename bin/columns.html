Columns Applet

by Ben Collins-Sussman
Available to all under the GPL version 2. Source code available on http://code.google.com/p/color-columns

How To Play:

  • Use the arrow keys to move the falling column and rotate the colors
  • Use the space bar to drop the piece
  • The object is to get 3-in-a-row of any color in any direction

Feel free to resize the window as large as you want; after closing the window, you can restart the game by clicking "reload" on your browser.
